# Analyst Memo - Shopify App Store Insights





#### **Dashboard:** Shopify App Store Analysis



#### **Reporting Period:** Latest available data



#### 

#### **Key Insight**



This analysis covers the reviews of various apps and their categories from the years 2018 to 2023 and how they reflect app performance. According to category comparison, the highest amount of ratings occur with Search Engine Optimization (SEO) apps. This tells us that the apps people use the most are ones that aid search engines.



#### **Business Impact**



When people search for listings, they primarily use keywords to find what they want. This means that they are utilizing SEO's most often because they help find desired products. As a result, SEO functionality and performance will likely determine Shopify app usage.



#### **Recommendation**



Focus on creating more apps that aid with SEO. According to our Trend Analysis, Apps Launched Over Time has steadily declined moving into 2023. Average ratings also declined slightly after reaching a peak in 2022. This tells us that the decrease in apps launched correlates to the decline in ratings. More apps that increase SEO usage will likely result in higher app traffic and an increase in ratings.
